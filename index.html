  • THEME

    Agriculture

    • Building smart and efficient solutions for farmers who are too far from knowledge and technology to increase the productivity and quality of crops grown.
    • Building platforms for direct chain supply of products from farmers to market.
    • Seed funding for smart farmers and local enterprise businesses idea based on agriculture and productivity.
    • Security system development for the farmers for their land and stock by developing small feasible product that are easily affordable.

    Education

    • Promoting education to all system by various technical means like smart classes for free etc.
    • Generalized school security systems that can include smart cameras, biometric system, face recognition etc.
    • Securing data of students and teachers using blockchain and making system flexible too for other fields in which it can be implemented.
    • Special portals and platform to educate senior citizens about various topics they required like mythology, cosmology current affairs etc.
    • Generalized apps for school/colleges to maintain data of various authority, teachers, students, feedback/review system, live classes over places etc.

    Efficient Energy Generation & Management

    • Making smart products for better use of energy at low cost of production.
    • Making basic facilities available to all like electricity, clean water supply. This can be done by local solutions like small, portable windmill system etc.
    • Using renewable resources for various purposes at low machinery cost.

    Health Care

    • Building optimized solution to get all high-end facilities from nearest hospitals.
    • Appointment booking and Medicine delivery system at doorsteps with automatic online payment.
    • Low cost machinery that can detect various kinds of diseases before they grow.
    • Maintaining a profile of a person related to medical history. So that he/she can be diagnosed faster in case of emergencies.
    • Tracking nearest hospitals, blood banks with units etc. in case of emergencies.

    Telesystem

    • A Communication system that can work on low bandwidth where network connectivity is too low.
    • Platform to store, update and verify data locally without using WWW and limit to perticular area .
    • Applications to compare prizes of various local shops including online shopping and payment portals.
    • E-Yellow Pages for all localities of a perticular village where data can be added and updated easily.

    Business & Entrepreneurship

    • Creating a platform for crowd sourcing and funding for potential startup and local traditional business.
    • To enhance local business and entrepreneurship skills by various online and offline solutions available on demand.

    Miscellaneous

    • Finding nearest NGOs, NPOs or govt. bodies for any help and support
    • Counselling for mental health and depression issues in locals.

  • FAQ

    Can I attend?

    Registration are open to all.Students(school/college),professionals,startups etc can surely participate.

    How does registration work?

    Registration is open until JAN 27th, 11:59 pm GMT+0530! Admissions will be based on a feasibility along with business espect of your idea. If you’re accepted, you’ll have to confirm your attendance by JAN 27th 2018 .

    Does this cost money?

    Nope! Admission is free and includes meals,drinks, snacks, overnight hosting,swag, and a memorable experience (if we do say so ourselves)!

    What will I eat?

    We’ll provide food for all meals from Monday’s breakfast to Tuesday’s breakfast..

    Where will I sleep?

    Hacking all night is fun, but you can also get some rest when you need it in our rest room!

    What if i reach venue before event and i need accomodation?

    We will start check-ins for paid accomodation by JAN 28th 6:00 pm GMT+0530 but you have to check-out by JAN 30th 12:00 pm GMT+0530.

    How do I get there?

    You can reach venue on your own convinience or reach to JODHPUR's selected checkpoints, JIET buses will pick you.Futher details will be given to you by mail if you are selected.

    What should I bring?

    Bring a valid student ID or government-issued ID for admission and a laptop for hacking. We’ll provide pretty much everything else—meals,snacks,toiletries, stickers, sponsor swag, and more! If you like to be comfy, you can bring a blanket, pillow, and towel.

    What about Internet?

    We will provide you free WIFI/LAN ,for better experience try to bring your own.

    How do teams work?

    Teams are at most 4 people. You can select teammates when you register, or you can register individually without specified teammates.

    What if I don’t have a team?

    If you don’t have a team now, that’s okay! We’ll have team formation and ideation events geared towards helping you find people to work with.

    What if I’m an JIET student?

    Registration are open to all students(school/college),professionals,startups etc can surely participate.

    What about hardware hacks?

    You have to bring your hack's hardware components.

    Where’s the schedule?

    We’ll release a more detailed schedule after closing date of registration and comfirmation.

    What are factions?

    We created factions to encourage hackers to have fun and interact with each other outside of normal teams when you’re not working on your projects. The Red Faction and Blue Faction are hackathon-wide groups that collectively complete challenges at RECKON-jiet 2018 for points. The faction with the most points will win and get a surprise upon the conclusion of the hackathon.

    How do I choose a faction?

    We will allot you faction during hall registration.

    Can team members belong to different factions?

    Yes, factions do not limit your forming of individual teams.

    What if this FAQ didn’t answer my questions?

    Please email us at email if you’re in any way confused or concerned! We’d love to help you out :)
